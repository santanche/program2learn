DISEASES AND THEIR SYMPTOMS
Format: disease(severity): symptom1, symptom2, ..., symptomN

flu(mild): fever, cough, headache, fatigue, muscle_aches
common_cold(mild): cough, sneezing, runny_nose, sore_throat
pneumonia(severe): fever, cough, shortness_of_breath, chest_pain, fatigue
covid_19(severe): fever, cough, fatigue, shortness_of_breath, loss_of_taste
measles(moderate): fever, cough, runny_nose, rash
strep_throat(moderate): fever, sore_throat, headache
heart_attack(critical): chest_pain, shortness_of_breath, nausea
anxiety(mild): shortness_of_breath, nausea
migraine(moderate): headache, nausea, sensitivity_to_light
food_poisoning(moderate): nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal_pain
allergic_reaction(severe): rash, swelling, difficulty_breathing
bronchitis(moderate): cough, chest_discomfort, fatigue, shortness_of_breath
mononucleosis(moderate): fatigue, sore_throat, fever, swollen_glands
